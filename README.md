# Zol

### About Zol

Zol is a Nashville-based promotional company that offers consumers a bundle of one-time-use exclusive discounts from top local bars, restaurants, and establishments. Zol uses barcode technology to facilitate the use of these discounts with our partner restaurants. Purchase a card in-app to unlock all our insane discounts.
